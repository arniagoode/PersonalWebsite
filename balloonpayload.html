High Altitude Balloon Payload

Role: Project Lead · Duration: Fall 2024 - Spring 2025 · Team of 10

Project Overview

Payload, nicknamed Riverhawk, was flown on a University of Maryland high-altitude balloon flight, reaching an altitude of over 90,000 feet. The onboard experiments and electronics included an automated robotic arm, a camera system, and an environmental sensing suite for atmospheric data collection.
