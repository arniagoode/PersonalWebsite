Linear and Rotational Motor Controller

Role: Highschool Student Researcher
Duration: Summer 2023

Project Overview

Designed and built a custom controller for a dual-axis motor system integrating both linear and rotational movements. The system precisely translated and rotated a tensioned optical fiber while synchronizing motion with an Aerosol Jet Printer head, enabling accurate deposition of ink to produce spiral patterns around the fiber.

Skills Developed
SolidWorks, Arduino IDE, C++, 3D printing, Soldering
